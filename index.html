About Me

Hello! I'm Derricka. I am currently enrolled in a Coding Bootcamp at Rutgers University. Although I don't have an extensive coding background, I did do AP and Honors Computer Science in High School as well as a couple of Computer Science related courses at Hunter College. It was something I enjoyed doing as a hobby and found quite amusing once I got the hang of things.

In the last two years since high school, I was working on my bachelor's degree in Chemistry. At the beginning of my junior year, I had to have surgery on my left knee; a surgery that ultimately left me with 8 screws in my femur and no meniscus. I was now unable to stand in a lab for hours at a time, a skill that is necessary in most Chemistry related careers. I decided to rekindle my long lost love and start coding again. Like most folks, my path into the coding world was an unexpected one but I am definitely glad to be here.
